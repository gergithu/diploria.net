Practical help

Turn a complicated situation into something you can act on.

I help you organize what’s happening, what matters, and what’s missing — so you can make the next decision confidently, and explain it clearly to someone else (a provider, a landlord, an insurer, a government office, HR, or even a family member).

Open a Guided Email → See the Case Study →

What you get

  • A clear summary (plain language).
  • A simple timeline (what happened, when).
  • A short “missing pieces” list (what to request or confirm next).
  • Optional: a draft message or letter you can send.

Who this helps

  • Personal processes: benefits, insurance disputes, immigration, housing/tenancy, consumer complaints, HR issues.
  • Families: aging-parent logistics, paperwork, coordinating with siblings/providers.
  • Small operators: contractors, consultants, small landlords, nonprofits — when you need a clear brief and next steps.

What I’m promising (and what I’m not)

I don’t promise “winning.” I promise clarity, structure, and usable writing — so you’re not improvising, rambling, or getting pushed around by paperwork.

If your situation requires legal or professional advice, I’ll say so. This is organization, drafting, and decision-support — not representation.
