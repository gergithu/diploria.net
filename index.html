This site traces a one–year experiment in pairing disciplined, executive-style judgment with modern AI systems. What began as a personal reef of notebooks, case files, and questions evolved into a full AI-assisted legal case study and a broader inquiry into how tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini can extend a single person’s reach.

Polyp by polyp, follow the one–year AI journey from first prompt to full casework.

A Year of AI

A structured, time-bound experiment: using AI to help dissect disclosure, compare evidence, track timelines, and draft professional communication in a real legal context.

The goal was not automation, but augmentation: testing how far a single person, working methodically, could go by pairing long experience with new tools.

• One self-represented case
• Multiple AI “engines” (polyps)
• Outcome: a complete, defensible narrative
