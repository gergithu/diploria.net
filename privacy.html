Privacy

Privacy & legal notes.

What this site does (and doesn’t) collect

  • This is a simple static site hosted on GitHub Pages and cached by Cloudflare.
  • I don’t run advertising trackers or analytics scripts.
  • The only personal information I see is what you choose to send me by email.

Email and storage

  • Emails go to a ProtonMail inbox, which is designed with privacy in mind.
  • I don’t share your emails or documents with third parties.
  • If I ever need to quote or reuse something, I’ll ask first and anonymize details.

Not legal or professional advice

Nothing on this site is legal, financial, or medical advice. I focus on structure, clarity, and drafting. If it looks like you need formal advice, I’ll say so.
